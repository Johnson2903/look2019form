About LOOK 2019

The theme of LOOK 2019 is ‘For this purpose’ with emphasis on Ps 40:7. A popular saying states that the beginning of recovery is discovery. While some often see Christian missions as a burden, but God designs His call to be a blessing to those He calls, as He did to Abraham (Gen 12: 1-2). However, lack of understanding of the attached blessings to the call often deprives a lot of Christians of their earthly and eternal blessings. Thus, there is a need for Christians to discover themselves in the will of God for them to be effective in the course of Christian missions.

LOOK 2019 will be opened with JE-HOSH-A-PHAT Mission Concert on Thursday August 15 (Day 1) by 5: 00 pm. The concert shall involve the launch of 2019 musical album project of FSM and presentation of the winners of Christian Music Missions Ministers’ Award. Morning session of Day 2 (Friday, August 16) will feature a lot of Training of Trainers events on the project arms of FSM till evening. Bro. ‘Dotun Adeboye, the National Director of Christian Missionary Foundation shall take over the stage in the later part of the event to minister on the theme of the conference.

Day 3 (Saturday, August 17) shall commence with the second aspect of the ministration by Bro. ‘Dotun Adeboye till around 10; 00 am. Thereafter, Glorious Future (a time to tap grace from testimonies of seasoned Christians that God has helped to successfully passed through challenging times) shall take place with a colloquium involving Prof. and Pastor (Mrs) Okunlola and Prof. Sunday Adigbo. After this the annual Re-union/ Thanksgiving of students and non-student members of FSM shall take place till 2:00 pm leading into Re-union lunch. Bible Study shall take place from 3: 30 – 5: 00 pm. The Annual leadership Congress shall run from 6:00 – 8: 30 pm.

As usual, the last day of LOOK 2019 (Sunday, August 18) shall be an opportunity for hands-on training on mobilization for missions among students till 11:30 am: Participants in groups shall visit Christian fellowships in Ibadan and Oyo towns. The event shall be brought to a close by 12:00 noon.

Meanwhile, the children component of LOOK 2019 shall run concurrently from Friday of the event till Saturday. The children in two categories (Primary and Secondary schools) shall be tasked on developing themselves in the Lord and skills for an excellent future.

By God’s grace, Medical and Spiritual Clinics shall take place all through the event, as well as social welfare at a certain period where interested brethren (especially students) shall have access to clothing and other useful materials donated by other members. Members are therefore implored to donate well washed fairly used or new clothing and other useful items for students.

Although participation in LOOK 2019 is free of charge and no offering is collected during the event, every participant is encouraged to be part of resource mobilization for the event. The unit cost per participant is N6,750 (covering accommodation, feeding and other logistics of the event, excluding transportation of participants to the venue).
